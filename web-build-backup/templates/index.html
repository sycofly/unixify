A comprehensive UNIX account UID and Group GID management system

Features

Centralized Management

Manage all your UNIX accounts and groups in one place with proper UID/GID validation.

Advanced Search

Quickly find accounts and groups with comprehensive search functionality.

Audit Logging

Track all changes with detailed audit logs for accountability and compliance. Access comprehensive audit history in each section.

Management Sections
